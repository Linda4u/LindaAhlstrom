Wheat Bread Linda Ahlstrom


Ingredients:

6 heaping cups wheat flour

Additional flour as needed

4 cups very hot tap water

3 heaping Tablespoons SAF instant yeast

2 Tablespoons salt

2/3 cup oil or shortening

2/3 cup honey or sugar

Optional:

2 Tablespoons dough enhancer

2 Tablespoons gluten

Combine flour, hot water, oil, sugar and salt. Mix well. Add yeast and

enough flour to form a soft dough (possibly 2 – 4 cups). If using optional

ingredients, combine with extra flour before adding to mixer.

Mix 10 min. Form into loaves and place in greased bread pans.

(Use four 8” glass bread pans or three 10” pans.)

Let rise to top of pans, about 30 min. Bake at 325 for 35-40 minutes

Refrigerator Hot Rolls Linda Ahlstrom


Combine and let stand 1 hour:

1 Tablespoon Yeast

1/2 cup lukewarm water

1 teaspoon sugar

Add: 2 teaspoons salt

1/2 cup sugar

1/2 cup butter

1 egg

1 1/2 cup warm water

5 to 5 1/2 cup flour

Mix together. Dough will be soft. Cover and refrigerate overnight or

Make rolls immediately. This dough can be used for cinnamon rolls.

Roll out on slightly floured board, and form into rolls. Place on well

greased pan. Let rise until double (about 1 1/2 to 2 hours).

Bake at 425 degrees for 12 to 15 minutes.

Frosting for Cinnamon Rolls Linda Ahlstrom


1/3 cup butter slightly softened

3 cups powdered sugar

Pinch salt

2 Tablespoons milk

1 teaspoon vanilla

Mix powdered sugar and butter. Stir in salt, vanilla and milk. Beat until smooth.

If spread on hot rolls, it becomes a glaze. If you wait for rolls to cool, it remains

a frosting.
