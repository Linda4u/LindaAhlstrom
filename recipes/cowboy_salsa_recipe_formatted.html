Cowboy Salsa

By Laura Mendenhall

6 medium tomatoeschopped
2 large avocadoschopped
1 bunch green onionschopped (I usually don't do all the green onions just about 3/4)
1 bag frozen sweet yellow corn(again just add it until you think it is enough)
1 can black beansrinsed and drained
1 bunch of cilantro leavesfinely chopped

Mix all that together and salt and pepper to taste also add a little lime juice (probably about 2 Tbls) to help keep the veggies fresh

Mix together

1 packet Good seasons Italian dressing
1/4 cup red wine vinegar
1/8 cup water

Shake really well then add

1/3 cup olive oil

Shake again then add to taste it is usually too juicy if you add all the dressing. Taste it and if you want more tanginess add more red wine vinegar also add more salt and pepper.
