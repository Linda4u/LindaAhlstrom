Green Salsa

By Ivonne Martinez

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. tomatillos
  • 2 Jalapeños*
  • 1 clove of Garlic
  • 1 medium onion chopped
  • ½ cup chopped cilantro
  • ½ teaspoon salt (heaping)

Instructions

  1. Peel tomatillos they will feel sticky as you are doing this. Wash tomatillos and jalapeños the tomatillos may still feel a little sticky but that is fine. Boil tomatillos and jalapeños in 2 cups of water in saucepan. Turn tomatillos and peppers over as they boil occasionally. When the color has changed on all sides remove from heat both the tomatillos and peppers will change color. Let this sit 5 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile peel 1 clove of garlic and place in the bender and chop cilantro and onion. Add tomatillos and jalapeños to the garlic in the blender with ½ teaspoon of salt this should be slightly heaping. Pour ½ cup of the water used to boil the tomatoes into the blender.
  3. Blend everything up but not too much blending. Add chopped onion and cilantro then serve.
  4. * This will be spicy the whole Jalapeño with seeds is used. To reduce spiciness try just one Jalapeño or remove seeds.
