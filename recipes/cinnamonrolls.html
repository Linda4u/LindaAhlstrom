Frosting for Cinnamon Rolls Linda Ahlstrom


1/3 cup butter slightly softened

3 cups powdered sugar

Pinch salt

2 Tablespoons milk

1 teaspoon vanilla

Mix powdered sugar and butter. Stir in salt, vanilla and milk. Beat until smooth.

If spread on hot rolls, it becomes a glaze. If you wait for rolls to cool, it remains

a frosting.
