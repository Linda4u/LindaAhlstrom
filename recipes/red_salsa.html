Red Salsa

By Ivonne Martinez

Ingredients

  • 5 Roma Tomatoes
  • 2 Jalapeños*
  • 1 clove of Garlic
  • ½ cup chopped cilantro
  • ½ teaspoon salt (heaping)

Instructions

  1. Wash tomatoes and jalapeños boil in 2 cups of water in saucepan. Turn tomatoes and peppers over as they boil occasionally. When the color has changed on all sides remove from heat both the tomatoes and peppers will change color. Let this sit 5 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile chop cilantro peel 1 clove of garlic and place it in the bender. Add tomatoes and jalapeños to the blender with the garlic add ½ teaspoon of salt this should be slightly heaping. Pour ½ cup of the water used to boil the tomatoes into the blender.
  3. Blend everything up but not too much blending. Add chopped cilantro and serve.
  4. *This will be spicy the whole Jalapeño with seeds is used. To reduce spiciness try just one Jalapeño or remove seeds.
