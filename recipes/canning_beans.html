Canning Beans

By Bev Monroe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of beans
  • 1 tsp salt
  • Hot water

Instructions

  1. In each canning jar add 1 cup of beans and 1 tsp salt. Fill jar up to the beginning of the neck with hot water leaving a one inch space before the lid. Put the flat lid and ring on the jar. Put jars in pressure cooker and fill the pressure cooker up with water to about 1 inch below the neck of the jars.
  2. Pressure can at 15 PSI for 90 minutes and then turn the heat off. Let cool and open when pressure has released (20-30 minutes).
