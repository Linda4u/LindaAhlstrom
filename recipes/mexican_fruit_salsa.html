Mexican Fruit Salsa

By Elizabeth Zelaya

Ingredients

  • 1 Mango diced
  • 1 Jicama diced
  • 1 Pineapple diced
  • 1 Cucumber diced
  • Small bag of Japanese peanuts
  • 1 Lime to taste
  • Chamoy to taste
  • Tajin to taste

Instructions

  1. Dice all fruit. Add lime chamoy and tajin to taste and mix together gently.
